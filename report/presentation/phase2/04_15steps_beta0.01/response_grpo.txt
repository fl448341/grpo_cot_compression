Problem (answer 27): Morisette and Kael were asked to bring fruits. Morisette brought 5 apples and 8 oranges, while Kael brought twice the amount of apples and half the number of oranges than Morisette. How many fruits do they have in total?

Morisette brought 5 + 8 = 13 fruits. Kael brought 2 * 5 = 10 fruits. Kael brought 1/2 * 8 = 4 fruits. Together, they had 13 + 10 + 4 = 27 fruits. #### 27.
